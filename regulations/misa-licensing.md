## Overview

The Ministry of Investment of Saudi Arabia (MISA) oversees foreign and domestic investment licensing. Companies must obtain appropriate licenses based on their business activities.

---

## License Types

### 1. Commercial License

**Description:** Required for companies engaged in commercial activities, trading, retail, and distribution within Saudi Arabia.

**Applicable Activities:**
- Import/export of goods
- Wholesale and retail trade
- E-commerce operations
- Distribution services
- Warehousing and logistics

**Article Reference:** Commercial Investment Law, Article 3, Section 1

---

### 2. Industrial License

**Description:** Required for companies engaged in manufacturing, production, or industrial activities.

**Applicable Activities:**
- Manufacturing facilities
- Production plants
- Assembly operations
- Industrial processing
- Factory operations

**Article Reference:** Industrial Investment Law, Article 5, Section 2

---

### 3. Professional License

**Description:** Required for service-based companies providing professional or technical services.

**Applicable Activities:**
- Consulting services (management, IT, engineering)
- Technical services
- Professional advisory services
- Software development and IT services
- Design and creative services
- Training and education services

**Article Reference:** Professional Services Law, Article 7, Section 1

---

### 4. Regional Headquarters (RHQ) License

**Description:** Special license for multinational companies establishing regional headquarters in Saudi Arabia to manage operations across the Middle East.

**Applicable Activities:**
- Regional management and oversight
- Strategic planning for regional operations
- Shared services (finance, HR, IT) for regional entities
- Regional procurement coordination
- R&D and innovation centers

**Article Reference:** RHQ Program Regulations, Article 2, Section 3

## Eligibility Criteria

### Commercial License

**Requirements:**
1. **Legal Entity:** Must establish a Saudi legal entity (LLC, branch, representative office)
2. **Minimum Capital:** SAR 100,000 (varies by activity)
3. **Business Plan:** Detailed business plan showing commercial activities
4. **Trade License:** Municipal trade license required
5. **Premises:** Physical office or warehouse location in KSA

**Documentation Required:**
- Company registration documents
- Shareholder agreements
- Memorandum of Association
- Proof of capital deposit
- Lease agreement for commercial premises
- Board resolution authorizing KSA operations

**Article Reference:** Commercial Investment Law, Article 3, Sections 2-4

---

### Industrial License

**Requirements:**
1. **Legal Entity:** Saudi legal entity with industrial classification
2. **Minimum Capital:** SAR 500,000 (varies by industrial activity)
3. **Facility Plan:** Detailed plans for manufacturing facility
4. **Environmental Compliance:** Environmental impact assessment
5. **Safety Standards:** Compliance with Saudi industrial safety standards

**Documentation Required:**
- Industrial facility plans and specifications
- Environmental clearance certificate
- Safety and compliance certifications
- Equipment and machinery specifications
- Production process documentation
- Quality management system documentation

**Article Reference:** Industrial Investment Law, Article 5, Sections 3-5

---

### Professional License

**Requirements:**
1. **Legal Entity:** Saudi legal entity (typically LLC or branch)
2. **Minimum Capital:** SAR 50,000 minimum
3. **Professional Qualifications:** Key personnel must hold relevant qualifications/certifications
4. **Service Scope:** Clear definition of professional services offered
5. **Insurance:** Professional indemnity insurance required

**Documentation Required:**
- Professional qualifications and certifications of key staff
- CVs and experience certificates
- Professional indemnity insurance policy
- Service delivery methodology
- Client reference letters (if applicable)
- Professional membership certificates (if applicable)

**Article Reference:** Professional Services Law, Article 7, Sections 2-4

---

### Regional Headquarters (RHQ) License

**Requirements:**
1. **Parent Company:** Must be an established multinational corporation
2. **Regional Scope:** Must manage operations in at least 3 countries in the region
3. **Minimum Investment:** SAR 1,000,000 minimum investment in KSA
4. **Key Personnel:** Minimum 15 qualified employees in KSA
5. **Strategic Functions:** Must perform strategic management functions

**Documentation Required:**
- Parent company financial statements (3 years)
- Organizational structure showing regional operations
- List of regional entities to be managed from KSA
- Business plan for RHQ operations
- Employment plan showing key hires
- Proof of office premises in KSA

**Special Benefits:**
- 100% foreign ownership
- Tax incentives
- Fast-track visa processing
- Exemption from Saudization for certain roles

**Article Reference:** RHQ Program Regulations, Article 2, Sections 4-7

## Common Requirements (All License Types)

### 1. Company Registration
- Commercial Registration (CR) from Ministry of Commerce
- Tax Identification Number (TIN) from ZATCA
- GOSI (General Organization for Social Insurance) registration

**Article Reference:** General Investment Law, Article 12

### 2. Saudization Compliance
- Minimum percentage of Saudi nationals in workforce
- Varies by sector and company size
- Monitored through Nitaqat system

**Article Reference:** Labor Law, Article 45; Nitaqat Program

### 3. Capital Requirements
- Capital must be deposited in Saudi bank
- Bank certificate required
- Capital adequacy varies by license type

**Article Reference:** General Investment Law, Article 8, Section 2

### 4. Physical Presence
- Registered office address in KSA
- Lease agreement or property ownership
- Municipal approval for premises

**Article Reference:** General Investment Law, Article 10

## Application Process

### Step 1: Preliminary Assessment
- Determine appropriate license type
- Review eligibility criteria
- Prepare initial documentation

### Step 2: Legal Entity Formation
- Register with Ministry of Commerce
- Obtain Commercial Registration (CR)
- Draft and notarize company bylaws

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks  
**Article Reference:** Company Law, Articles 2-5

### Step 3: MISA License Application
- Submit application through MISA portal
- Upload required documentation
- Pay application fees

**Timeline:** 4-8 weeks  
**Article Reference:** Investment Law, Article 15

### Step 4: Additional Registrations
- ZATCA (tax registration)
- GOSI (social insurance)
- Municipality (trade license)
- Chamber of Commerce membership

**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks

### Step 5: Final Approvals
- Receive MISA license approval
- Obtain stamped documents
- Open corporate bank account

**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks

**Total Estimated Timeline:** 9-17 weeks

## Compliance Checkpoints

### For RFP/Contract Analysis

When analyzing a business document, check for:

**✓ Business Activity Description**
- Is the core business activity clearly stated?
- Does it match any specific license type?

**✓ Legal Structure Mention**
- Is there mention of establishing a legal entity?
- What type (branch, LLC, representative office)?

**✓ Investment Amount**
- Is there indication of capital investment?
- Does it meet minimum requirements?

**✓ Operational Scope**
- Where will operations be based?
- What is the geographic scope?

**✓ Personnel/Staffing**
- How many employees planned?
- Are there Saudization considerations?

**✓ Physical Facilities**
- Is there mention of office, warehouse, or factory?
- Location specified?

## Common Gaps in Applications

### Missing Documentation
- Incomplete financial statements
- Missing professional certifications
- Unclear business activities description

### Insufficient Capital
- Capital below minimum threshold
- No proof of capital deposit

### Unclear Business Model
- Activities don't match license type
- Multiple license types needed

### Compliance Oversights
- No Saudization plan
- Missing environmental clearances (industrial)
- No professional insurance (professional services)
